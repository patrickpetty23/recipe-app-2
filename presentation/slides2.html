The Problem

Cooking is harder than it should be

1

Recipes are scattered

Screenshots, Instagram saves, bookmarked blogs, hand-written notes — no single place to find what you want to cook tonight.

2

Nutritional awareness is zero-effort... or zero

People either obsessively track every gram in a separate app, or have no idea what they're eating. Nothing in between.

3

Cooking alone is stressful

Constantly glancing at a screen, losing your place, forgetting timers. Nobody is guiding you through the process in real time.

Our Solution

One app.
Every recipe.
Zero friction.

Mise combines AI recipe capture, voice-guided cooking, auto-generated visuals, and nutrition tracking into a single, beautifully designed mobile app.

  • Capture any recipe in under 10 seconds
  • Hands-free, voice-guided step-by-step cooking
  • AI-estimated nutrition — no manual input ever
  • One-tap Walmart cart — search, match, and send
  • AI weekly meal planner with nutrition tracking

Appendix — Technical Stack

Full Technology Overview

Frontend

  • React Native (Expo SDK 54)
  • Expo Router v6 (file-based routing)
  • expo-linear-gradient (hero images)
  • expo-speech (TTS narration)
  • expo-keep-awake (cooking mode)
  • PanResponder (swipe gestures)
  • Animated API (smooth transitions)

Data & Storage

  • expo-sqlite v16 (WAL mode)
  • FK cascade constraints
  • Parameterised queries (SQL injection safe)
  • Offline-first — no cloud DB
  • cook_log + recipe_nutrition tables
  • Background migrations on boot

AI / Cloud

  • GPT-4o — recipe extraction, chat, nutrition
  • DALL-E 3 — hero thumbnails (1792x1024)
  • DALL-E 2 — step illustrations (parallel)
  • JSON mode for structured output
  • 45s / 60s timeout guards
  • Non-blocking background pipeline
